# 32 Brand Guidelines

## Name

**Divini Procure.** Part of the Divini family (sibling: Divini Partners). Always
two words, both capitalized. Domain: **diviniprocure.com**.

## Positioning

The transaction and trust layer for real estate procurement: developers source
**verified** construction vendors, run RFQs, compare bids, and award work in one
place. The promise to developers is "everyone here is licensed and insured."

## Voice

- **Premium, direct, conversion-aware.** Confident, not hype-y.
- **Free-first and fair.** Lead with what is free; make the fee logic feel just
  ("we protect" the existing relationship at 1% vs "we created" the new one at 2%).
- **Concrete over vague.** Use real numbers ("2% capped at $2,500",
  "$10M -> $50,000"), never fuzzy claims.
- **Honest about the gate.** The verification badge is a documentation-and-tracking
  claim, not a guarantee. Never overstate it.

## Copy rules (hard constraints)

- **Zero em dashes.** This is a project-wide convention enforced across code,
  docs, and copy. Use commas, parentheses, or periods instead. (This OS follows it.)
- **Avoid** the phrases "AI Procurement Platform" and "Subscription Software" in
  public copy (per the pricing-page voice notes).
- Featured and Enterprise/upsell offers are always framed as **optional**, never
  as gates or memberships you must buy.
- No setup fees / seat fees / paywalls to post a project, claim a profile, or get
  matched. Say so plainly.

## Visual identity

- **Primary color:** deep emerald (`--emerald` #1E5D4A / `--emerald-deep` #123c2e).
- **Accent:** champagne (`--champagne` #D9CCB0), used for the brand mark.
- **Type:** Cormorant Garamond (serif) for display/headings; Inter for body/UI.
- **Feel:** calm, editorial, generous whitespace, restrained status color. See
  `31_DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`.

## Pricing language (canonical)

- Developers: Free, forever.
- Vendors: Free to join, 5 bids per quarter; Vendor Pro $149/mo for unlimited.
- Success fee: "Only pay when you win. 2% of the award, capped at $2,500."
- Grandfathered: "Already working together before Divini? You stay at 1%, capped
  at $1,000. We didn't create the relationship, we protect it."
